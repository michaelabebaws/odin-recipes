Shrimp

Discover the succulent delight of Shrimp, a seafood treasure that adds a pop of flavor and vibrancy to any dish. With their delicate, snappy texture and rich, slightly sweet flavor, shrimp are a versatile culinary gem. Whether savored in a zesty seafood paella, a spicy Asian stir-fry, or simply grilled to perfection with a squeeze of lemon, shrimp elevate every meal into a mouthwatering experience.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp (1-2 pounds)
  • Butter/Olive Oil (2-3 tbsp)
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps to prepare shrimp

  1. Rinse: Rinse the shrimp under cold water to remove any impurities.
  2. Peel: Peel the shrimp, removing the shell and tail.
  3. Devein: Remove the dark vein that runs down the back of the shrimp.
  4. Clean: Clean the shrimp under cold water to remove any remaining bits of shell or vein.
  5. Pat Dry: Pat the shrimp dry with paper towels to remove excess moisture.
  6. Cook: Mix seasons (salt & pepper) with butter or olive oil.
