Stake

Savor the luxury of a perfectly cooked Steak, a culinary masterpiece that delights the senses. A tender cut of high-quality beef, seared to lock in juices and grilled to desired doneness, unleashing a rich, beefy flavor profile. Whether you prefer the buttery softness of a Filet Mignon, the robust heartiness of a Ribeye, or the lean sophistication of a Sirloin, every bite of a well-crafted steak is a gastronomic indulgence.

Ingredients

  • Steak (USA, Japan, etc)
  • Salt & Freshly Ground Black Pepper
  • Cooking Oil/Fat (Olive Oil, Avocado Oil, or Butter)

Steps to prepare stake

  1. Bring to Room Temperature: Remove the steak from the refrigerator and let it sit at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  2. Trim Excess Fat: Trim any excess fat from the edges of the steak.
  3. Season: Season the steak with salt, pepper, and any other desired seasonings.
  4. Pat Dry: Pat the steak dry with paper towels to remove excess moisture.
  5. Oil: Brush the steak with oil to prevent sticking.
  6. Sear: Sear the steak in a hot skillet or grill to lock in flavor and texture.
