Lasagna

Indulge in the rich flavors of Italy's beloved dish, Lasagna. This classic comfort food is a masterful layering of tender lasagna noodles, rich meat sauce infused with herbs, a blend of creamy ricotta and melted mozzarella cheese, and the subtle zest of parmesan. Each bite transports you to the Tuscan countryside, satisfying your senses with its hearty, satisfying goodness.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles (8-10 sheets)
  • Meat Sauce (homemade/store-bought)
  • Mozzarella (2 cups, shredded)
  • Parmesan (1 cup, grated)
  • Ricotta (1 cup)

Steps to prepare lasagna

  1. Cook Noodles: Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Prepare Meat Sauce: Cook ground beef and tomato sauce to create a meat sauce.
  3. Prepare Ricotta Mixture: Mix ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, and egg.
  4. Assemble: Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish, followed by a layer of noodles.
  5. Add Ricotta Layer: Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  6. Add Cheese Layer: Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Repeat Layers: Repeat steps 4-6.
  8. Bake: Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-40 minutes, or until golden brown and bubbly.
